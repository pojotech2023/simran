Offline Courses

1

FASHION DESIGNING

Fashion Designing courses that cater to aspiring designers at various stages of their career. Our Diploma, BSc, and MSc in Fashion Designing are designed to offer both theoretical knowledge and practical experience, preparing students to excel in the dynamic fashion industry.

Diploma in Fashion Designing (1-Year Duration): Our Diploma in Fashion Designing is a comprehensive 1-year program aimed at students who wish to kickstart their career in fashion.

Eligibility: 10th and Above

BSc in Fashion Designing (3-Year Duration): Our BSc in Fashion Designing is a 3-year undergraduate program that provides an in-depth understanding of fashion design and its practical application in the industry. This course combines creativity with technical expertise, covering various aspects of fashion such as garment production, pattern drafting, and the business of fashion.

Eligibility: 12th and Above

MSc in Fashion Designing:For those looking to deepen their expertise in fashion and design, our MSc in Fashion Designing offers a postgraduate qualification that blends creativity with advanced design techniques. This program is ideal for students who aspire to work in senior design positions or launch their own fashion brand.

Eligibility: Any Degree

Boutique Management (6 months)

Boutique Management Course at Simran Institute is designed to equip you with the skills needed to successfully manage and run a boutique. In this comprehensive program, you’ll learn key aspects of boutique operations including inventory management, customer service, marketing strategies, and financial planning.

MEHENDI (HENNA ARTIST)

Mehendi Course at Simran Institute offers expert training in the art of Henna design, from traditional to modern styles. In this hands-on course, students will learn intricate mehendi patterns and techniques, enabling them to create beautiful designs for weddings, events, and more. Personalized instruction ensures quick mastery of this ancient art.

5

AARI EMBROIDERY

Aari Embroidery Course teaches the delicate art of intricate hand-embroidery using the Aari needle. Students will master various techniques to create stunning, detailed designs for garments, accessories, and home décor. Personalized training ensures a thorough understanding of this traditional craft.
