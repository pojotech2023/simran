About Us

ABOUT SIMRAN INSTITUTE

Welcome to Simran Institute, managed by Simran Educational Foundation since 2010. As one of the best and top-rated fashion institutions, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive education and vocational training that empowers students to excel in the fashion industry and beyond.

Offline Courses: With a focus on high-quality, hands-on training, we offer a range of specialized courses, including Fashion Designing, Tailoring, Boutique Management, Mehendi Henna, and Aari Embroidery.

Online Degree Programs: At Simran Institute, we go beyond vocational training. We offer online degree programs affiliated with renowned universities and state boards. These programs include 10th and 12th grades in Science, Commerce, and Arts, making education accessible to all, no matter where you are located.

At Simran Institute, we believe in nurturing talent and providing students with the tools they need to achieve their career goals. Join us today and become part of a legacy of successful professionals in the fashion and vocational industries.

Contact Us Today to learn more about our programs and start your journey toward success!
